Facts about Airplanes

Airplanes were invented in 1903 by the Wright Brothers in Kitty Hawk, Kentucky. Their first airplane was called the "Wright Flyer", and it flew 120 feet (37 meters) and the flight lasted on 12 seconds.

Airplanes have developed alot since then, and now most airplanes are capable of flying hundreds to thousands of kilometers in just a few hours. America, Germany, France, UK, and Russia were the main pioneering nations of Aviation.
